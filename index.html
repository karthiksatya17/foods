    Idly Recipe:


Idly Recipe,how to make soft Idli Batter using rice or rava.


    Making soft and fluffiest Idlis at home is really an art which you can easily
  

    master with my step by step recipe guide. In this post I share 2 ways to make 


    healthiest and softest Idli at home. The first one is using rice and the other
      

    is using idly rava, which is also known as Rice Rava  in some parts
       
 
    of the world. Both the methods will give you soft idlis.

Ingredients:

  1. Idli Rice-Short-grain pearly white rice makes the perfect idli


  2. Skinned Gota Urad Dal-Although you can also use the skinned urad dal it still often has traces of the outer black skin which can make the batter look discolored


  3. Poha-ingredient but I would highly recommend it as these flattened rice flakes add lightness to the idli. If you do not have poha you can substitute it with idli rice


  4. Methi Seeds-seeds help with the fermentation process
